TSOCIETY

Translators and proofreaders, native speakers, ready to boost your global communication!

  • Translators Network

    The diversity of our group of translators allows us to work in a wide variety of fields. We have availability 24/7 in order to satisfy your linguistic needs.

  • Linguistic Quality Assurance (LQA)

    To ensure quality, each text we handle undergoes three mandatory steps: translation, editing, and proofreading. This means every single project is reviewed by, at least, three different linguists.

  • Experience and Expertise

    Due to our long history of working with many partners around the world, we specialize in texts from a wide variety of fields, such as Life Sciences, IT and Telcom, Marketing, Gaming, Tech and more.

  • Commitment to Security

    Maintaining the confidentiality of your information is paramount. That’s why we sign confidentiality agreements with all our partners and have reliable security systems to ensure the safety of your information while we work on your projects.
